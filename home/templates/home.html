Admission Requirements
  1. The approved text-books as contained in the recommended list of books.
  2. Exercise books for junior students includes 5 long notes, 5-80 leaves, 5-60 leaves 15-20 leaves. While for senior include 15 long notes and 15-20 leaves
  3. Revised Standard Version Bible, Ancient& modern and student manual Assembly.
  4. A pair of black container shoe, Navy blue beret for girls, a pair of white socks and white canvas
  5. A wooden locker and wooden back chair (please no iron locker or Seat)
  6. 1 packet of white board maker (black), 2 brooms, 1 ream of A4, 1 Big Tissue
    N/B: Please note that all these are subject to review in subsequent times . Also all the items required must be presented before admission
things not PERMITTED
  1. Students must not bring personal items such as toys, radio, cameras,
  2. phones, games, and any sharp objects etc.
  3. No indiscriminate use of drugs, if a child is on medication the school must be informed.
  4. Improper dressing. dirtiness and lateness
